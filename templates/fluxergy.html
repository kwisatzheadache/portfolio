2018 - 2022: Fluxergy

While at Fluxergy, I worked on several challenging and impactful projects. One of my primary responsibilities was the development of a fullstack application to manage clinical data, which allowed for multiple input sources and generated multiple reports. Additionally, I designed and implemented a shop floor system for production management that facilitated data input by engineers and utilized data egress ETL to ELK and PowerBI.

Another key project involved algorithm development, specifically the main PCR analysis algorithm. This required the use of computer vision and parameterized curve fitting to create a robust and accurate algorithm.

As Head of the Data and Algorithms Department, I was responsible for overseeing the development and implementation of these projects, as well as managing a team of engineers and ensuring the successful integration of these new systems into the company's operations. Overall, these projects were instrumental in improving data management and analysis at Fluxergy, and I'm proud to have played a key role in their development and success.
