2018 - 2022: Fluxergy

Fluxergy had it all: Science, passion, and creativity! Fluxergy is building a multi-modal point-of-care diagnostic device. PCR, lateral-flow, cell-cytometry, all on one test card.

I was the third member of the software team - what a time to join! I got to build out the first two major data systems. I took both systems through multiple iterations while the company learned more about its actual needs. So much of this work was on-the-ground requirements gathering, shadowing multiple departments and carrying workloads through the process with the actual users. I think I learned more about the intersection of business, users, and software development here than anywhere else.

Once the data systems were mature enough to pass on, I moved onto algorithm development. I got promoted to lead the New Algorithms Department, spearheading an overhaul of our main PCR algorithm to meet FDA requirements. Convergence of hardware, softare, manufacturing and medical software requirements made this an exciting computer vision problem. I am proud to reflect on the fact that my algorithm is powering the next-generation of medical diagnostics.
