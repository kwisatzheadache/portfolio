03 : About me

Who am I?

I'm Michael. Born in Kansas, but I've lived all over. If you want a chronology, it's something like this: Kansas -> Pennsylvania -> Kansas -> Colorado -> Missouri -> Kansas -> Germany -> Florida -> New York -> Kansas -> California -> Croatia -> Kansas

What's my favorite? That's a tough one - before I moved to California, I probably would have said Germany. But now, I've come to love hiking, climbing, camping, and general wilderness-ing, so I'm probably going to settle somewhere a little more wild.

What are your values?

I'm a simple man. I like to work hard, but it's gotta mean something.
The world is changing - there are so many places to make a positive impact.

Soil health and permaculture are huge interests. How can we make farming more accessible? The average age of the American farmer is 58. We're gonna need a lot more farmers very soon. (It's kinda like what they said in "Interstellar"). How can software contribute to tackling present and future food-scarcity issues?

What (computer) languages do you speak?

Python, SQL, Kotlin, C++, (a bit of) Typescript. SQL (is it really a language? Well, it's right there in the name).

Why Software?

Software is the union of two of my favorite things: Math + Poetry

What are your goals?

I'd like to homestead.
But as far as software is concerned, I'm happy making impactful contributions to any meaningful project.
