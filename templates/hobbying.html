Hobbying

Ah, some of my all-time favorite climbing excursions include the exhilarating crack climbing in Moab, which always provides a challenge and beautiful scenery to enjoy. Another highlight was tackling the difficult and awe-inspiring Epinephrine route in Red Rocks, which tested my skills and endurance to the limit. And then there's the stunning Flower of High Rank, which was an incredible and unforgettable experience that showcased the absolute best of what climbing has to offer.

Apart from my passion for climbing, I also enjoy playing the guitar and piano in my free time. I find it a great way to unwind and express myself creatively. I also love taking my dog Owen for long walks and exploring the outdoors together, whether that means going for a run or hitting the trails for a day of hiking. Additionally, I enjoy trail running, which is a great way to stay in shape and experience the beauty of nature at the same time. Lastly, I have a love for freediving, which allows me to explore the underwater world and feel a sense of peace and serenity.
